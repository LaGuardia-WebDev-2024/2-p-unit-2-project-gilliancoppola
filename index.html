Why You Should Visit the City of Rome, Italy

By Gillian Coppola

Rome is the beautiful capital of Italy, located in the Lazio Region. It is in the Central-Western part of Italy, so it is not truly "North" or "South" like many other cities and regions in the country.

Why visit Rome? Well, Rome is an amazing city full of historic architecture and breathtaking art. Not only are the sights gorgeous, but Rome has plenty of food to enjoy, from pizza to pasta to unique desserts and snacks.

Places to visit in Rome

  1. The Vatican
  2. The Pantheon
  3. The Trevi Fountain
  4. Piazza Navona
  5. The Roman Forum
  6. The Colosseum

Explore Rome!

But be warned! Don't let street vendors scam you into buying bracelets or roses--they're never free. And make sure you plan accordingly because the buses are SO SLOW.
